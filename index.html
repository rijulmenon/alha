The ALHA Story

Crafted with love, presented with elegance

Our Beginning

Born from a passion for perfection

ALHA began in a small home kitchen in Kerala, where every dessert was a labour of love made for family and friends. What started as handmade gifts evolved into something far greater — a luxury catering atelier that now graces weddings, corporate galas, and intimate celebrations across the UAE.

We believe a dessert isn't just something you eat. It's a memory you carry. Every piece we craft is designed to be tasted, remembered, and talked about long after the last bite.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

✦

Uncompromising Quality

We source only premium-grade ingredients — single-origin chocolates, fresh Kerala spices, real saffron. No shortcuts, ever.

◇

Artisan Craft

Every piece is hand-made, hand-finished. Our pastry team brings years of classical training to each creation, blending French technique with South Asian heritage.

❋

Bespoke Experience

No two events are alike. We work closely with each client to design a dessert spread that reflects their personality, culture, and vision perfectly.

⬡

Elegant Presentation

The visual is as important as the taste. Our tablescapes, packaging, and plating are designed by aesthetes who treat every event as an editorial spread.

Cultural Fusion

We blend the richness of Keralan sweetcraft with French pâtisserie and Levantine confectionery — creating flavours that feel both familiar and extraordinary.

Warm Hospitality

From first consultation to final bite, we treat every client like family. Our service is as memorable as our desserts.

How We Grew

Our Journey

2022

The First Kitchen

ALHA starts in a home kitchen in Kollam, Kerala — making custom cakes and dessert boxes for family celebrations and word-of-mouth orders.

2023

First Wedding Commission

A 400-guest wedding in Kochi becomes our defining moment. The dessert table earns a feature in a regional lifestyle magazine.

2024

Expanding to the UAE

We open our Dubai atelier and begin catering to luxury hotels, corporate clients, and the emirate's most celebrated weddings.

2026

500 Events & Growing

With over 500 events crafted and a team of dedicated pastry artists, ALHA continues to redefine what luxury dessert catering means in the region.
